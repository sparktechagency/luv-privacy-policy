Introduction

Our Commitment to You

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the LUV: Link Up Ventures app. We take the protection of your personal information very seriously. By using our app, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

01

Information We Collect

📋 Personal Information

Name, email address, phone number (collected during registration), profile photo, date of birth, and gender.

📍 Location Data

We collect your precise and approximate location. Purpose: to power nearby user discovery and matching features within the app.

📷 Camera & Media

Camera and media access is requested for taking profile photos or uploading images from your gallery. Your photos are never shared with third parties without your consent.

🔔 Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications for new matches, messages, and app updates.

📢 Advertising ID

We use the Google Advertising ID (AD_ID) to display relevant advertisements and for app analytics purposes.

💳 Payment Information

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. We do not directly store any card or payment details on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display relevant advertisements
  • We never sell your personal data to any third party
